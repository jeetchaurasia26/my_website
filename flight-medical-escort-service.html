Medical Escort Services

What Is a Medical Escort Service?

A medical escort service provides a trained medical professional who travels with the patient by ground vehicle or by air (commercial or dedicated aircraft) to monitor, treat, and support throughout the journey.

Medical Escort in Air Ambulance

In non-critical cases, patients may be fit to fly seated on a regular passenger flight. However, they may still require accompaniment by a medically qualified professional to monitor their health and ensure safety. Air Medical 24X7’s network of qualified and experienced medical escorts provides high levels of personal attention and professional care throughout the journey.

Getting the right professional medical escort can be extremely challenging, as vetting credentials and experience is critical. Choosing a medical escort provider like Air Medical 24X7 ensures the patient safely reaches their destination without hassle or medical discomfort.

From specialist doctors to experienced nurses, our medical director recommends the most suitable escort after assessing the patient’s medical history, current condition, and fit-to-fly status. Escorts are equipped with necessary medical equipment and medications, ensuring bed-to-bed service from hospital to hospital, hospital to home, home to hospital or home to home.

Why Have a Medical Escort?

Air transfer can quickly move patients from medically underserved areas to regions or countries with higher-level medical facilities and specialized care. Integral to this process is having a skilled medical team accompany the patient.

The medical escort team can include doctors, nurses, and flight medics who bring all required medications and equipment. During the flight, they administer medical procedures, observe vital signs, and provide comfort, empathy, and personal care.

Relief & Personal Attention

Having experienced medical professionals onboard brings immense relief to patients who may be anxious, overwhelmed, or traveling without friends or family. Air Medical 24X7 medical escorts are qualified, compassionate, and empathetic, offering 1:1 monitoring, medication administration, and continuous care.

Medical Escorts carry essential medical supplies and equipment (service suitable for stable, non-critical patients), ensuring comfort and safety throughout the journey. Our focus is on personalized care and attention for every patient.
