What Is the Cost of an Air Ambulance Charter?

The cost of an Air Ambulance Charter can vary significantly based on several important factors. Understanding these variables can help you better estimate expenses and choose the right service for your needs. Key factors influencing the air ambulance cost include:

  • Distance to be Covered: The overall price depends on pickup and drop locations.
  • Type of Aircraft: Jets, turboprops with varying capacities.
  • Onboard Medical Equipment: Advanced ICU care increases cost.
  • Medical Staff: Doctors, medics, and nursing team add cost based on needs.
  • Ground Ambulance Arrangements

Our pricing is always transparent. Whether you need a full ICU air ambulance or a medical escort on a commercial flight, we provide options to suit all budgets.

1. What is an air ambulance charter?
An air ambulance charter is a private aircraft equipped with ICU facilities and medical staff, used to transfer critically ill or injured patients from one location to another. At Air Medical 24X7, we offer 24X7 charter services worldwide.

The cost of an air ambulance in the UAE typically ranges from ₹1.5 lakhs to ₹25 lakhs. The final price depends on several factors, including:

  • Services included
